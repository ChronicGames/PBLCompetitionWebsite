About Us

Hello, welcome to Clothes R' Us. We are a non-profit organization with the sole purpose of providing people in need with gently used clothing. There are many ways to express yourself and tell someone that you care. We think that giving someone comfortable and warm clothing is a great way to help not just them, but yourself. Feel free to stop by the store anytime that we are open. Our hours are listed below.
