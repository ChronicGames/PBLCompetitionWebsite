Kid Zone

Welcome to our Ball Pit! This is located in the KidZone part of the store and is only available to kids. We do have an adult present at all times to ensure that the kid's remain safe. The ball pit is 20 feet in width, 25 feet in length, and the areas range from 1-5 feet in depth. We fully sterilize our ball pit three times a week to ensure that your kids have a sanitary environment to play in every day. We do however, conduct other less time-consuming sanitation methods on a daily basis.


Welcome to our Pool Hall! There are eighteen tables in total. We don't want the kids to have to worry about taking turns so we have aquired multiple tables. If you would like to play a couple games with your kids we will allow that so long as there are not too many children present at that moment. We do this because we do not want adults hogging the tables. They are primarily for the kids.


Welcome to our Art Studio! We have a room and plently of art supplies if your little munchkin loves art. We also have a couple art majors for the older kids, if they want some lessons.
