Clothes R' Us Closet

Hello, welcome to the Clothes R' Us Closet. This is an area of our store that is completely dedicated to people in need. This area can be accessed anytime that we are open. Simply walk in and ask to visit the Closet. Every single piece of clothing in our Closet is free. We understand that people go through hard times and trying to figure out how you can afford a brand new jacket in the winter can be a problem, especially if you have kids. We have coats, jackets, hoodies, short-sleeve shirts, long-sleeve shirts, pants, shoes, socks, and hats. If you are in need of clothing, please feel free to stop by the store anytime and take a look around. We have sizes to fit all individuals, whether you are a small or an XXX-L. We also have a specific area for kids clothing.
